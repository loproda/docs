## Submit reports

Visit [Submit an abuse report](https://abuse.cloudflare.com/) to submit a report.

## View submitted reports

Users with Admin, Super Admin, or Trust & Safety roles can view any abuse reports submitted and accepted against the content associated with their account.

1. Log in to the [Cloudflare dashboard](https://dash.cloudflare.com/login).
2. Select **Manage Account** > **Abuse Reports**.
3. Optionally, filter the reports based on date, report status, report type, and domain.

## Receive notifications

You can enable Trust & Safety notifications for your account for information about new blocks, removed blocks, or updates related to content. 

For help setting up alerts, refer to [Configure Cloudflare notifications](/notifications/get-started/).
