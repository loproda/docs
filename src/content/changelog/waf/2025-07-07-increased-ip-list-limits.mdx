---
title: Increased IP List Limits for Enterprise Accounts
description: We have significantly increased the limits for IP Lists on Enterprise plans to provide greater flexibility and control.
products:
  - waf
date: 2025-07-07
---

We have significantly increased the limits for [IP Lists](/waf/tools/lists/) on Enterprise plans to provide greater flexibility and control:

- **Total number of lists**: Increased from 10 to 1,000.
- **Total number of list items**: Increased from 10,000 to 500,000.

Limits for other list types and plans remain unchanged. For more details, refer to the [lists availability](/waf/tools/lists/#availability).
